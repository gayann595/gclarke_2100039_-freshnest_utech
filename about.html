About FreshNest 🌿

Learn about who we are, what we stand for, and the person behind this project.

🏪 Our Business

FreshNest is an all-in-one accessible e-commerce platform combining a bakery, grocery store, and laundry service. We serve everyday Jamaican households with fresh, quality products delivered to their door.

🎯 Mission Statement

To provide fresh, affordable, and convenient shopping and home services to all Jamaican households with a commitment to full accessibility for persons of all abilities.

🌟 Vision Statement

To be Jamaica's leading inclusive e-commerce platform where every customer, regardless of ability, can shop with ease, dignity, and confidence.

👥 Intended Audience

Everyday Jamaican households with a special focus on persons with physical, visual, hearing, and cognitive challenges who are too often overlooked by traditional e-commerce platforms.
